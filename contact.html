About me

I'm Aneeth Kumaar from Erode, Tamil Nadu, India. Right now I'm down in Kerala studying Math for my BS-MS at IISER Thiruvananthapuram. Math is great for the logic side of things, but down the line, I want to pivot into robotics or anything to do with physical hardware. On the creative side, I'm obsessed with graphic design and film, specifically cinematography. I usually volunteer to handle design work for a lot of campus clubs. I was once the design head for CMIT (our math club) and spent a lot of time designing yearly newsletters and asset packs across campus.

When I'm away from a screen or a project, I'm usually playing volleyball or relaxing with video games ranging from open-world adventures like Lego Marvel Superheroes to single-player campaigns like Ghost of Tsushima.
